Playlist Mirror

Explanation of Required Permissions

View your Spotify account data

Your email

  • As this app becomes basically once you set it up, it otherwise would not be able to notify you about errors like expired Spotify account access.

Your name and username, your profile picture, how many followers you have on Spotify and your public playlists

  • (User-) name to display it to you on the website so you know who you are signed in as.
  • Playlist access to see whether you deleted a mirror playlist, so the app can stop mirroring.
View your activity on Spotify

What you've saved in Your Library

  • Without knowing what's in your library in the first place, the app can't mirror it to another playlist :)

Playlists you've made and playlists you follow

  • To check whether you deleted a mirror playlist, so the app can stop mirroring.
Take actions in Spotify on your behalf

Create, edit and follow private playlists

  • To create new mirror playlists and be able to write songs to them.
